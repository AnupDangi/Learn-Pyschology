## Courses

### Yale PSYC 110 — Introduction to Psychology

**Professor Paul Bloom · Open Yale Courses**

20-lecture introductory course covering the full breadth of scientific psychology — from neuroscience and development to social behavior, mental illness, and happiness.

→ [Course folder](./Introduction%20to%20Psychology%20%28PSYC%20110%29/)  
→ [Download complete notes PDF](./Introduction%20to%20Psychology%20%28PSYC%20110%29/PSYC110_Complete_Notes.pdf)  
→ Course website: https://oyc.yale.edu/psychology/psyc-110

### DiMAP — Diploma in Modern Applied Psychology

**Achology · Udemy**

Practical, applied psychology diploma covering self-awareness, cognitive frameworks, behavioral patterns, and personal development — delivered as PDF worksheets and reflective exercises.

→ [Course folder](./Diploma%20in%20Modern%20Applied%20Psychology%20Course%20%28DiMAP.%29/)
