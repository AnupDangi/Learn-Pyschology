A practical, applied psychology diploma focused on self-understanding, cognitive frameworks, behavioral change, and personal development. All materials are PDF worksheets and reflective exercises designed to be worked through actively, not just read.

## How to Use This Folder

This folder contains **44 PDF resources** grouped below by theme. Work through them in the suggested order, or jump to any theme that matches your current focus.

> **Tip:** Start with the `DMAP Course Journal - NEW.pdf` to track your reflections as you go through the materials.

## Suggested Study Path

1. **Start:** `DMAP Course Journal` + `Developing a Healthy Mindset for Reflective Study`
2. **Foundation:** Self-awareness section (Johari Window, Core Identity, Evaluating Yourself)
3. **Cognitive tools:** Cognitive Bias, Cognitive Distortions, Ladder of Inference, ABC CBT
4. **Deeper work:** Psychodynamic section (Defense Mechanisms, Jungian Archetypes, Unconscious)
5. **Growth:** Mindset section + Carl Rogers materials
6. **Integration:** Social influence section + self-reflection exercises
7. **Ongoing:** Update your course journal throughout
